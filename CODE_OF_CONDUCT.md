# Contributor Code of Conduct

Our community is dedicated to providing a harassment-free experience for everyone. We ask that contributors adhere to the following expectations:

- Be respectful and considerate in communication.
- Use inclusive language and avoid insults or derogatory comments.
- Assume good intent and offer constructive feedback.

Reporting
If you experience or witness behavior that violates this code of conduct, please report it to the maintainers at: sany2k8@gmail.com

Enforcement
The maintainers will review reported incidents and may take actions such as removing content, restricting contributors, or closing issues/PRs depending on severity.

This Code of Conduct is a living document and may be updated by the maintainers as needed.
